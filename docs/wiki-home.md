# Alternity Warship Generator Wiki

Welcome to the **Alternity Warship Generator** wiki — a desktop app for building Warships and Stations/Bases for the [Alternity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alternity) sci-fi tabletop RPG, implementing the construction rules from the *Warships* sourcebook by Richard Baker.

## About the App

The generator walks you through a step-by-step wizard covering all aspects of ship or station design:

1. **Hull** — Choose ship class or station type
2. **Armor** — Select armor type and weight
3. **Power Plant** — Set your energy budget
4. **Engines** — Configure sublight propulsion
5. **FTL Drive** — Add faster-than-light capability
6. **Support Systems** — Life support, accommodations, gravity, stores
7. **Weapons** — Beam, projectile, torpedo, and special weapons
8. **Defenses** — Shields, ECM, and countermeasures
9. **Sensors** — Detection and targeting arrays
10. **Command & Control** — Bridge and communications
11. **Hangars & Misc** — Shuttle bays, cargo, and extras
12. **Damage Zones** — Assign systems to hull zones
13. **Summary** — Review and export to PDF

Designs can be saved as `.warship.json` files and exported to PDF for use at the table.
